Information Science and Engineering

The Department of Information Science Engineering at NMAMIT offers a B.Tech. degree in ISE. The program is designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills necessary to design, develop, and manage information systems. Students learn about the theoretical foundations of information science and engineering, as well as the practical skills needed to work in the field.

The curriculum of the B.Tech. in ISE program includes courses in the following areas:

  • Computer science
  • Information theory
  • Data structures and algorithms
  • Database systems
  • Software engineering
  • Information security
  • Human-computer interaction
  • Machine learning
  • Artificial intelligence

In addition to the core courses, students also have the opportunity to take elective courses in areas such as big data analytics, cloud computing, and cyber security.
The Department of ISE at NMAMIT is well-equipped with modern laboratories and facilities. Students have access to state-of-the-art computing systems, as well as a variety of software tools and resources.
The department also has a strong faculty with extensive experience in teaching and research. The faculty members are actively involved in research in areas such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data mining.
The B.Tech. in ISE program at NMAMIT has a good placement record. In the past few years, the average salary package offered to ISE graduates has been around 8-10 LPA.
If you are interested in a career in information science or engineering, the B.Tech. in ISE program at NMAMIT is a good option. The program will provide you with the knowledge and skills you need to succeed in this rapidly growing field.
