Computer Science and Engineering

  • The CSE department at NMAMIT was established in 1986 and is one of the oldest and most sought-after departments in the institute.
  • The department offers a 4-year B.Tech. degree in CSE.The department also offers a B.Tech. degree in CSE with specializations in Computer Science (Full Stack Development), Computer Science (Cyber Security).
  • The curriculum of the CSE program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the fundamentals of computer science, as well as the skills and knowledge required to pursue careers in a variety of fields, such as software development, data science, and artificial intelligence.
  • The department has a highly experienced and qualified faculty who are committed to providing students with the best possible education.
  • The department also has a well-equipped laboratory with state-of-the-art facilities, which allows students to gain hands-on experience in various areas of computer science.
  • The CSE department at NMAMIT has a strong placement record. In the 2022 placement season, the highest salary package offered to a CSE student was INR 53 LPA.

Overall, the CSE program at NMAMIT is a good option for students who are interested in pursuing a career in computer science. The department has a strong faculty, good infrastructure, and a good placement record.
Here are some of the key highlights of the CSE program at NMAMIT:

  • The program is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).
  • The department has a student-faculty ratio of 15:1.
  • The department has a placement cell that helps students secure jobs in top companies.
  • The department has a well-equipped laboratory with state-of-the-art facilities.
  • The department offers scholarships and financial assistance to deserving students.
